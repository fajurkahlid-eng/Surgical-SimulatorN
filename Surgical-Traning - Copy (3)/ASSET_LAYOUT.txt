Surgical Training — asset folders (PlayCanvas Editor or self-hosted)

Place GLB files under your PlayCanvas project’s asset bucket (Editor: drag into Assets panel).
Suggested local mirror for version control:

  models/
    mainbuild.glb      — hospital / building environment (static mesh + colliders recommended)
    doctor.glb          — doctor mesh (optional visible body); player root is usually an empty + camera
    BodyHuman.glb      — patient on bed

Optional:
  models/needle_and_thread_-_inktober.glb  — decorative UI or future 3D needle prop (not required for 2D suturing mode)

In the Editor, after import, assign model entities under your "World3D" hierarchy (see setup instructions bundled with the scripts).
